Primary purpose of the site is to enable alternate learning for young / special children who require additional visual aid to make sense of the world around.
Most available apps and games have flashy elements to them which make it attractive for young children to interact with them even when they are making the wrong choices.
For that reason, this tones down the reinforcement aspects of wrong/correct answers in favor of parents giving them verbally / in person.
These choices make the application parent oriented - to be used as a teaching aid more than a play thing.
